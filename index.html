Bio

I'm currently a second-year PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Duke University, supervised by Prof. Neil Gong. Prior to that, I earned my Bachelor's degree from the University of Science and Technology of China in 2024.

My research focuses primarily on AI Security. Recently, I am mainly focused on the following areas:

  • LLM agent security, such as prompt injection attacks to web agents.
  • Federated learning, including poisoning attacks, defenses, and aggregation algorithms.
